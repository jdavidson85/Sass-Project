Baseball Is Back

After a nail-biting 99-day stalemate, the MLB and the MLBPA have come to a tentative agreement, allowing baseball to begin its 2022 season and it'll look slightly different than it did in 2021.

Ken Griffey Jr and Derek Jeter are two of my favorite baseball players of all time. These two individuals inspired millions of young kids to play baseball. I've collected a lot of their baseball cards myself and have always loved these two athletes for their raw ability to play the game of baseball. I loved the way Ken Griffey Jr Swung the bat and those majestic home runs he hit. I loved the way Derek Jeter played defense and how he would go deep in the infield to throw runners out at first base.
